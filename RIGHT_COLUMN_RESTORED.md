## Why You Might Not See It

If the right column appears empty or dark:

1. **Scroll Down**: The content might be below the fold
2. **Browser Width**: Make sure your browser is wide enough (use wide layout)
3. **Theme Issue**: Try toggling between light/dark mode
4. **Cache**: Clear browser cache and refresh (Ctrl+F5)
5. **Zoom Level**: Reset browser zoom to 100%
